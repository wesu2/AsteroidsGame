Asteroids Game

Press "A" to turn counter clockwise
Press "D" to turn clockwise
Press "W" to acclerate
Press "S" to stop
Press "F" to hyperspace
Press "R" to shoot bullet
When your ship hit a meteror, the meteror will disappear, but two more will respond, so make sure to break it with bullets instead of your own ship
Make sure to conserve your bullets
The Meteror will only break by your bullet if you hit the middle point of the meteror, so remember to aim
